Girl Child Education & Empowerment

To achieve gender equality for our girls, empowerment is very key for us.
The depth of your creativity and the solution you can produce to ease a problem is a constant if you must stand up to great competitiions
Despite the background of the girls in our community, we owe them elimination of all forms of violence and discrimination; we owe them elimination of any form of harmful practices and protection from child marriage.
We are committed to empowering girls with the right knowledge to enable their full and effective participation in opportunities for leadership and decision making in public life, politics and the economy.

The trauma of poverty and inequality is more damaging to our girls than the sickness of malaria.....
But, with the right knowledge, right skill, right empowerment, right orientation, exposure to life building abilities and problem solving technology, our girls will champion Equality and Excellence in their chosen fields....

For the sake of one, reaching out is worth it! If you Will, Join Us...

Global Goals

Priceless Virtues International Initiative, a non-governmental organization that advocates for the girl-child right to formal education, empowerment and STEM education. We are concerned on the girl-child to be able to improve her self-esteem, find her a voice and her right to education.

Peace, Justice & strong institutions

The high level of armed violence, sexual violence and insecurity have a destructive impact on the girl child. They deserve to be tutored, protected and loved. Goal 16 of the SDG's is dedicated to the promotion of peaceful and inclusibve societies for sustainable development, the provision of access to justice for all.

Good health & Well-being

For us at Priceless Virtues; Good Health and well being for our girls also means Health and Hygiene when they are on their menstrual period. From our several interactions with the girls in our community, one thing is common; Period Poverty. Girls struggle to stay clean when they are on their period...
Some of the Girls save up money and stay hungry to stay clean during their Menstrual period....
